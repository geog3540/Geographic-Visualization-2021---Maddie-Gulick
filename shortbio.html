Maddie Gulick Bioscience and GIS

I am a senior at the University of Iowa studying bioscience and geographical information systems. Many of my interests focus on the environment and the methods we can take to protect it. I am currently working alongside Oregon State University and the Big Fish Lab to explore the movement and aggregation patterns of white sharks off the coast of California. After graduation, I will be pursuing a research project using drone and GIS technology to assess the health and diversity of subaquatic vegetation in Okoboji, Iowa.
